FCC-style exhibit · engineering review required. Genoa is a deterministic propagation studio. AI may explain and draft narrative; it never calculates contours, never overrides warnings, and never claims FCC approval. Filing is the licensed engineer's signature, not the assistant's.
§73.183 · §73.184 · §73.313 · §73.333 · §74.1204 Three layers, one source of truth. A deterministic FCC method below, scientific evidence beside it, an AI assistant above. The numbers come from the engine. The story comes from the engineer.
Layer 1 · Source of truth

Official FCC method

FM F(50,50) & F(50,10) per §73.313 / §73.333. AM groundwave per §73.183 / §73.184. LPFM & translator service contours per §73.811 / §74.1204. Reproducible from a pinned curve dataset with sha256-hashed provenance.

Layer 2 · Evidence

Scientific data

Terrain DEM, FCC facility records, antenna patterns, ground conductivity, SDR captures (SigMF), waterfall snapshots, RDS / RadioDNS / EAS-SAME identity, calibrated measurement logs with uncertainty bands. Evidence is evidence; it is not authority.

Layer 3 · Narrative

AI assistant

Drafts engineering English, explains anomalies, summarizes contour-vs-measurement deltas. Cannot calculate contours. Cannot override warnings. Cannot certify a filing. The engine remains the citation.
