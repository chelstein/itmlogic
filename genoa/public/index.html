Genoa FCC Propagation Studio · Tack 1

AM · FM · LPFM · FM Translators
Carry the signal farther on a single tack.
Standalone tool. Genoa runs on its own service (Express + Postgres + DO Spaces) and is decoupled from buoyIQ and any other Alliance system. Upstream repos (itmlogic, splat, ZTRpsITS, SigMF, EAS-Tools, massdns, zerotrustradio) are consumed read-only as data sources for kernels, terrain, station catalog, and SDR measurements.
§73.183 · §73.184 · §73.313 · §73.333 · §74.1204 A clean contour, by lamplight… Three layers, one source of truth. A deterministic FCC calculator, a scientific-evidence engine fed by terrain DEM & live SDR measurements, and an AI assistant that explains anomalies and drafts exhibits — but never replaces the math.
Layer 1 · Source of truth

Official FCC method

FM F(50,50) & F(50,10) per §73.313 / §73.333. AM groundwave per §73.183 / §73.184. LPFM & FM-translator service contours per §73.811 / §74.1204. Every number reproducible from the published curves.

Layer 2 · Evidence

Scientific data

Terrain DEM, FCC facility records, antenna patterns, ERP / HAAT, ground conductivity (M3), land cover, SDR captures (SigMF), waterfall snapshots, RDS / RadioDNS validation, and timestamped measurement logs with uncertainty bands.

Layer 3 · Narrative

AI assistant

Flags bad inputs, explains contour-vs-measurement deltas, drafts exhibits in plain engineering English, generates maps. The deterministic calculator above remains the citation.

Backend data sources

Read-only consumers. Genoa never writes to any of these systems; it only pulls reference kernels and measurement records. Genoa's own state lives in its own Postgres + Spaces.

kernel · host
chelstein/itmlogic
ITS Irregular Terrain Model
Pure-Python ITM port; canonical kernel for batch radials, p2p links, and uncertainty bands on the F(50,10) curves. Genoa lives in this repo.
kernel
chelstein/splat
Terrain-aware coverage (SPLAT!)
Longley-Rice ITM contour overlay on USGS / SRTM DEM. Drives the supplemental terrain map and HAAT verification radials.
kernel
chelstein/ZTRpsITS
ITS p2p / area mode wrapper
Reference-grade ITS propagation toolkit (P.2P, area). Used to validate itmlogic outputs against NTIA reference cases.
read-only
chelstein/SigMF
SDR measurement schema
SigMF recordings (IQ + metadata) are the canonical evidence format for the measurement appendix; timestamped, geo-tagged, uncertainty-bounded.
read-only
chelstein/EAS-Tools
Audio / SAME validation
Confirms the measured signal really is the licensed station (RDS PI/PS, EAS LP-1/LP-2 chain audibility, audio fingerprint).
data only
chelstein/zerotrustradio
Facility DB & SDR catalog
Source of FCC AM/FM/LPFM/translator facility records and SDR measurement metadata. Never modified by Genoa — buoyIQ operations are unaffected.
read-only
chelstein/massdns
RadioDNS resolver
Resolves fm/<gcc>.<pi>.<freq>.radiodns.org to confirm hybrid-radio identity matches the FCC facility record.
